engine --> builder : 1.6 new TestSuiteBuilder(...)

' === Генерация чрез DI и ABC алгоритъм ===

generator --> outputGenerator : 2.2.2 GenerateOutput(...)

generator --> builder : 2.3 return HashSet<TestCase>
